# XLC-ROLL-001 — Living Company Commercial Rollout

**Status:** Approved implementation  
**Authority:** Darren Cureton  
**Release:** 1.0.0  
**Public gateway:** `solutions.html`

## Commercial outcome

The rollout must create revenue from the governed manual family while using that product as inspectable proof of Xen's broader Living Company capability. The manual offer and the strategic offer are connected but remain independently understandable and independently accessible.

## Canonical funnel

1. **Executive documentary** — creates understanding, participation, and executive conviction.
2. **Standalone publication family** — allows a buyer to inspect the complete suite or any individual manual without replaying the documentary.
3. **Manual license** — establishes a product-level commercial entry point.
4. **Department system license** — packages the nine connected publications as one operating system.
5. **Guided implementation** — adapts, installs, trains, measures, and governs the operating system.
6. **Living Company Blueprint** — maps company truth before a broader operating-memory pilot is proposed.

## Single-gateway rule

The rollout publishes one canonical Xen Solutions Gateway URL. BDC deployment, Living Company Blueprint, and Checkmate comparison are internal states of that gateway, not separate campaign URLs. The documentary preserves a viewer's selected path in session state and opens the same canonical gateway at the appropriate indexed section. Separate public links are reserved for genuinely separate products—the nine individual manuals and the complete XBM suite.

## Three appointment continuations

| Documentary choice | Commercial continuation | Required working session |
|---|---|---|
| Complete the BDC proof | Deploy the complete BDC system | BDC Deployment + Measurement Session |
| Build 1 Living Company | Design one truthful company-layer pilot | Living Company Blueprint Session |
| Compare Checkmate 1 | Inspect one fair use case | Checkmate Operating-Layer Comparison |

The choice-specific close must never activate the final appointment narration or completion state without explicit viewer activation.

## Publication access contract

- Every manual has a durable direct route.
- The complete suite has one canonical family route.
- Public proof links open the full governed publication; no summaries are represented as the paid master.
- Downloadable masters, toolkits, updates, customization, and implementation remain licensed deliverables.
- The gateway may not invent pricing, publish personal contact information, or imply an active checkout/calendar provider until Darren authorizes and configures those services.

## Second-appointment privacy boundary

The public gateway asks the visitor to select an intent, not disclose company-sensitive information. It generates a working-session brief locally in the browser. Private company discovery begins only in the authorized second appointment and inherits the applicable security controls.

## Conversion rule

Every major surface provides one obvious next action:

- documentary → chosen proof or Solutions Gateway,
- manual → complete suite or gateway,
- gateway → manual inspection or appointment path,
- appointment path → portable meeting brief,
- completed premiere → Solutions Gateway with the selected path preserved.

## Pending commercial connections

These integrations require explicit provider details before activation:

- payment/checkout provider,
- approved prices and license terms,
- scheduling provider and calendar owner,
- approved public sales/contact address,
- CRM or lead-routing destination.

The gateway is deliberately provider-neutral so these connections can be activated without redesigning the experience.
