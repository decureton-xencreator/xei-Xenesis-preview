# XDBS–Xenesis Preview Integration

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active Preview Contract  
**Canonical architecture:** `decureton-xencreator/xen-operating-system/architecture/xdbs/XDBS-001-XEN-DAILY-BREAD-STUDIO-ARCHITECTURE-v1.0.md`

## Purpose

This contract defines how the public Xenesis executive preview may demonstrate XDBS capabilities without misrepresenting unimplemented live services.

## Allowed preview surfaces

- Daily Bread executive landing-page layout;
- Academy Command cards;
- World in 5 Minutes story cards;
- Water Cooler bullet cards;
- Sports, Entertainment, and XMI preview modules;
- travel, weather, markets, risks, opportunities, and execution cards;
- Xen Globe route and location visualization;
- responsive XPS/XDL navigation and interaction patterns.

## Required labels

Preview-only, simulated, static, or future capabilities must be labeled clearly. The preview may not imply live geolocation, live flight status, automated publication, personal-data access, or real-time market/news ingestion unless those services are connected and validated.

## XPS requirements

All preview surfaces must inherit:

- mobile-first responsive design;
- accessible typography and touch targets;
- reusable studio components;
- consistent visual language;
- tappable source links where real reporting is shown;
- privacy-aware demonstrations;
- clear operational-versus-concept boundaries.

## Product relationship

Xenesis may expose Daily Bread as a flagship executive entry surface. XDBS remains governed by XPS and its canonical architecture in the private operating-system repository.
